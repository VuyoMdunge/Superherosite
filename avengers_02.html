Iron Man

Bio

Irons man's full name is Tony stark.He was the son of is the wealthy son of industrialist and weapons manufacturer Howard Stark and his wife, Maria. Tony grew up a genius with a brilliant mind for technology and inventions and, naturally, followed in his father’s footsteps, inheriting Stark Industries upon his parents’ untimely death

After being kidnapped after a weapons test, he had to use his intelligence to build the Mark 1 Iron man suit. After this incident he pulled away from weapons but still worked on developing the iron man suit.After a few months he met Nick Fury was asked to start the Avengers Team.
